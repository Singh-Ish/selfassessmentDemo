Instructions to access the portal:

  1. To access the Self-assessment website, you need to be on Carleton Network. If you are off-campus, you will need to use VPN .
  2. Please user your cmail email account to login to the application
      -sample: "****@cmail.carleton.ca"
  3. Once you submit the log in request, an email will be sent to your registered Id.
    • If you think you are an authorized user but still can't log in, kindly contact to Admin staff: saportal@sce.carleton.ca to have access to the portal
    • No email will be sent to any unauthorized Email Id's
  4. It uses a token-based validation, and the token will be valid for 10 minutes. If the token expires, you have to request it again.
  5. Once you have successfully logged into the portal, you will be redirected to the Student dashboard, which will have all the information about you and your respective group.
    • If there is any incorrect information, please reach out to the Admin staff: saportal@sce.carleton.ca to address the issue before submitting the assessment.
    • You can also share your feedback about the portal at the end of the assessment.
